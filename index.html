Some commonly
asked questions

What should I look for in a scented candle?

The three most important things to look for in a candle are its wax, wick and ragrance. The highe rthe quality of ingredients, the more well-crafted and valuable the candle

How long does the scent of a candle last?

Highly fragrant candles have a lifespan of 6 to 12 months, depending on the scent. If you enjoy scented candles, the use and replace/ refill them to avoid deteririation in appearance and performance.

How do I know if my candle is high quality?

Quality candles durn cleanly, evenly, and create a pool of liquid wax across the surface of the candle within 2 to 4 hours.

How do i choose a fragrance?

The easiest way is ti actually pick them up and smell them. Some thinks to ponder while at it are: Does the fragrance make me feel good? Does it remind me of anything? Would wearing it make me happy? (most candle fragrances are in perfumes)
